Privacy Policy for RetroTAP

Last Updated: March 8, 2026

1. Introduction

ACALI Studios ("we," "our," or "us") operates the mobile game RetroTAP. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Game.

2. Data Collection and Use

Local Data: RetroTAP stores game-related data, such as high scores, player initials, and game settings, locally on your device using industry-standard persistence (SharedPreferences). This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Personal Data: We do not collect personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number.

3. Third-Party Services

The Game uses Google Play Services, which may collect information used to identify you. We encourage you to review the Google Play Services Privacy Policy.

4. Children’s Privacy

RetroTAP is an arcade game intended for a general audience. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
